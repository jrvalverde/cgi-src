GRAMM low resolution docking

This form allows you to predict putative docking among a 'receptor' and its 'ligand' using the Global Range Molecular Matching approach of Ilya A. Vakser. For more details and citation, see the references below.

The computer will perform a LOW RESOLUTION molecular docking with default parameters. This may yield unrealistic predictions. You are well advised to look on to these results cautiously.

This method is advised when you have inaccurate or low resolution structures or wish to overcome the multiple minima problem. Parameters used in the computation are those suggested for globular proteins and ligands bigger than 50 atoms.
